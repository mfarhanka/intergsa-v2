Company Background

A logistics business shaped by freight forwarding experience and steady operational expansion.

The company was founded in June 1994 as Monday Transport Service Sdn. Bhd. and was renamed Inter GSA Freight Services Sdn. Bhd. in August 1998 as it expanded deeper into local and international logistics operations.

Its legacy positioning emphasizes consolidation shipments, door-to-door services, bonded transport support and overseas partner relationships that extend delivery capability beyond Malaysia.
