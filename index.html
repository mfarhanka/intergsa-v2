Company story

From Monday Transport Service to Inter GSA Freight Services.

The business was founded in June 1994 as Monday Transport Service Sdn. Bhd. In August 1998, it became Inter GSA Freight Services Sdn. Bhd. as the company expanded its logistics and freight forwarding focus across local and international operations.

The company highlights consolidation shipment, door-to-door services, bonded transport capability and partnerships with overseas offices as key parts of its operating model.
